### What's happening with Baby

The heart is beating at nearly 150 beats per minute! Facial features like the tip of the nose are forming.

### What's happening with You

Morning sickness might be at its peak. Focus on hydration and small, frequent meals.

### The "Plan" Strategy

This is the week most parents book their first official prenatal appointment. Start a folder (digital or physical) to keep all your medical records in one place.
